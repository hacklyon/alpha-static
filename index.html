FAQ

What is HackLyon?

HackLyon is a student run hackathon held at Doua campus. It will take place on March 24th and 25th.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event in which computer programmers and others in the field of software development, like graphic designers, interface designers and project managers collaborate intensively on software-related projects.

Who can apply?

If you are a student and over 18 you are welcome to HackLyon! If you graduated less than a year ago, you are very welcome to!
